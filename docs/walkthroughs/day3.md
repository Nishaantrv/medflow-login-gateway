# Day 3: Doctor Agent & Clinical Workflow

Phase 3 focused on the clinical center of the application: the Doctor's workspace.

## 🩺 Doctor Dashboard
- **Schedule Management**: A comprehensive view of daily clinical appointments with status tracking.
- **"My Patients" View**: A dedicated portal to manage assigned patients, view histories, and clinical markers.

## 📝 Clinical Tools
- **AI SOAP Note Generator**: A powerful tool that takes raw clinical inputs and organizes them into structured **S**ubjective, **O**bjective, **A**ssessment, and **P**lan notes.
- **Vitals Integration**: Automated structured data extraction from clinical conversations.

## 📊 Technical Implementation
- **Relational Joins**: Complex queries to link appointments with patient profiles and medical records.
- **Role Redirection**: Logic to ensure doctors are greeted with their specific clinical context upon login.
