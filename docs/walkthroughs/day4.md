# Day 4: Hospital Admin & Family Portals

Phase 4 extended the system to the broader hospital ecosystem, improving operational transparency and family inclusion.

## 🏢 Hospital Admin Agent
- **Bed Management**: A real-time grid visualizing bed occupancy across ICU, General Ward, and Specialty units.
- **Admissions Flow**: Integrated "Admit Patient" dialogs to assign patients to available hospital resources.
- **Staff Directory**: Filterable directory of all medical and administrative personnel with shift status.
- **Operational Dashboard**: Real-time stats calculation for admissions, discharges, and occupancy.

## 👨‍👩‍👧 Family Portal
- **Patient Monitor**: Plain-language updates on a loved one's clinical status, translating complex jargon.
- **Vitals Tracking**: Interactive health pulse section showing HR, BP, and SpO2 in real-time.
- **Visit Scheduler**: Dedicated tool for families to coordinate visits according to hospital policies.
